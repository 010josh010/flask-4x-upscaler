About This App

This is a simple Flask application that lets you upload an image or video and upscale it using Real-ESRGAN.

What’s Included

  • Image and video (MP4/MKV) upscaling with live progress
  • Pause/Resume and Terminate controls for long video jobs
  • Shared stylesheet via Flask’s static folder, with template inheritance
